DONOR RECOGNITION


All donor recognition should seek to offer three important things to the donor audience: access, information, and recognition. All development communication should celebrate KEXP's relationship to a particular donor or business sponsor. Donor recognition is as much about acknowledging their contribution's impact on the success of the organization and its mission, as much as it is meant to express gratitude to the donor for their generosity.

STEWARDSHIP BEST PRACTICES


Stewardship involves managing gifts as donors intend, and updating donors on the progress and impact of their gifts. A thoughtful onboarding campaign to welcome new donors keeps them plugged in and reminded of your mission. Regular transparent reports of where donations go reinforces the impact of their donations and increases their awareness of your ongoing needs. Actions of gratitude, like thank you messages, should reflect the organization's excitement, appreciation, and care for their support and partnership. Gratitude comes off as more genuine when actions are expeditious and focused on the donor, not the specific donation. Regularly send updates to keep donors informed and updated of organizational activities. Offer next steps for how they can remain involved. Doing so will help cultivate a long-term, mutually-beneficial relationship.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT PROCEDURES

Project Launch

Assemble all necessary technical team members and key stakeholders to conduct the initial project kick-off meeting. Meet with the goal to define and initiate a project strategy.

Requirements & Design

Define the scope of the project design deliverables with input from subject matter experts, business operations, and technical designers.

Planning

Establish key objectives and outline the development of work needed to achieve end goals. Schedule work for development and execution.

Execution

Conduct iterative development and collaborate daily with necessary team members responsible for key project deliverables through final deployment or publishing. Manage resources to maintain the project's timeline and budget for defined deliverables.

Deployment

Make a key event, communication, or deliverable accessible to users or target audience. A/B test communication or design to measure interest and engagement.

Analysis & Review

Collect, visualize, and share data associated with implementations of the project deliverables to measure success or weaknesses of the design, communication, or tooling. Review findings with team members and stakeholders.
